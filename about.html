About UNC Computer Science

The computer science program at UNC offers a dynamic and innovative learning environment, providing students with a comprehensive education in cutting-edge technologies. Led by world-class faculty, the program emphasizes hands-on learning experiences, collaboration, and problem-solving skills development. Through internships,clubs,and student organizations, UNC computer science students gain practical experience and networking opportunities that prepare them for successful careers in the rapidly evolving field of technology.

Mission Statement

Our mission at UNCoded is to support and guide computer science students through their academic journey at UNC Chapel Hill. We are dedicated to fostering a community where every student can thrive, offering mentorship, resources, and guidance to navigate the complexities of the computer science program. Through collaboration and empowerment, we aim to cultivate a diverse and inclusive environment that nurtures the growth of future leaders and innovators in the field of computer science
